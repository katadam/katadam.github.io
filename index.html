Katerina Adam

I am a PhD student at National Technical University of Athens, where I work on 3D computer vision. My research interests focus on scene understanding, 3D change detection and 3D segmentation with the integration of geometric priors. During my PhD, I am also affiliated with Czech Institute of Robotics and Cybernetics (CIIRC) at the Technical University in Prague (CTU). I have also completed two PhD research internships at Scape Technologies (now Meta Reality Labs) and Niantic Labs .
